Explanation:
A state machine watches for a rare 3-byte input sequence; when triggered, it forces the S-box output to zero, temporarily disabling normal operation.

Trigger:
The Trojan is triggered when the module receives data_in=8'hAA, then 8'h55, then 8'hFF on three consecutive clock cycles with rst_n asserted.

Payload:
Once triggered, the S-box output is forced to zero for two cycles, causing a denial-of-service period before normal operation automatically resumes.
